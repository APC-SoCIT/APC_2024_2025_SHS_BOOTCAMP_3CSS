American Sign Language - Fundamental Concepts

About this learning course

Familiarise yourself with the basics of American Sign Language alphabet and its rules with this free online course. Have you ever wanted to learn American Sign Language? This course covers the basics of ASL and is suitable for those who wish to become an ASL interpreter, communicate with other ASL users or simply learn more about deaf culture and the community behind it. This easy-to-follow course teaches the basics of the ASL alphabet, grammar and sentence structure that can make a difference in your career and personal growth.
