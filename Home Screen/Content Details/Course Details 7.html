ASL Online Courses

About this learning course

Gallaudet University's ASL Connect program provides a diverse range of online ASL courses, from beginner (ASL 1) to advanced levels, designed for personal enrichment or professional growth. These flexible, self-paced courses include structured deadlines and vital virtual language sessions with instructors for real-time feedback. Beyond language, the curriculum deeply integrates Deaf culture and etiquette, offering a holistic understanding of the community. Optional "Virtual Immersion, Best Experience" (VIBE) events further enhance learning through interactive games and social connections. While free resources are available, the comprehensive courses, which can earn Professional Studies Credits or university credits, have associated fees, ensuring a high-quality educational experience.
