ASL 1 - Unit 1 Sample

About this learning course

In this free sign language unit sample from our comprehensive ASL 1 course, you'll dive directly into the fundamentals of American Sign Language. This introductory module is meticulously designed to provide a foundational understanding, beginning with the most basic signs essential for everyday communication. You'll gain practical exposure to how these individual signs coalesce into meaningful exchanges by observing a conversation in action, demonstrating the natural flow and rhythm of ASL. This isn't just about memorizing vocabulary; it's about seeing the language come alive through context and interaction. The unit aims to build your confidence and equip you with the initial tools needed to start engaging with the deaf community.
