GG Learns FSL: Emergency Phrases

About this learning course

Learning Emergency Phrases in Filipino Sign Language (FSL) is crucial for effective communication during critical events like earthquakes and other disasters. These phrases empower Deaf individuals to convey urgent needs and receive vital information, bridging a critical communication gap in emergencies. Resources are available, often through video tutorials and community initiatives, to teach signs for concepts like "earthquake," "help," "danger," and "safe." Familiarity with these signs can significantly improve safety and coordination for the Deaf community and first responders alike. Prioritizing the widespread dissemination and learning of these FSL emergency phrases is a vital step towards inclusive disaster preparedness in the Philippines.
